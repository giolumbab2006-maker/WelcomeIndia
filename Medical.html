Medical & Wellness Innovation

Healing the World

India has emerged as a global healthcare powerhouse, combining affordable high-tech medical care with ancient holistic healing. As the world's largest provider of generic medicines and a pioneer in digital health, India is redefining what it means to lead a healthy life in the 21st century.

The Pharmacy of the World

India is the largest provider of generic drugs globally, fulfilling over 50% of the world's demand for various vaccines. With world-class Biotech hubs in Hyderabad and Pune, Indian scientists are at the forefront of affordable cancer research, insulin production, and pandemic response.

Advanced Medical Tourism

Every year, thousands travel to India for complex surgeries, from robotic heart transplants to advanced oncology. India’s JCI-accredited hospitals offer world-class outcomes at a fraction of the cost found in the West, supported by a vast pool of highly skilled surgeons.

Ayush: The Holistic Heritage

The Ministry of AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy) has standardized ancient practices for the modern world. From the Yoga Capital of Rishikesh to the Ayurvedic retreats of Kerala, India offers a preventative healthcare model that focuses on the balance of mind, body, and spirit.

The Digital Health Stack

Through the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, India is creating a digital health ID for every citizen. This infrastructure allows for seamless tele-consultations, digital prescriptions, and the world’s largest government-funded healthcare scheme, ensuring no citizen is left behind.
