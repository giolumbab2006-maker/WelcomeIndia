Amber Palace

A Regal Citadel of Jaipur

Perched atop a rugged hill overlooking the bustling city of Jaipur stands the magnificent Amber Fort (or Amer Fort). Commissioned by Maharaja Man Singh I in 1592, this UNESCO World Heritage site is a grand fortress that serves as a timeless witness to Rajasthan's illustrious past.

Sandstone Splendour

Built from a harmonious blend of pale yellow and pink sandstone, the fort exudes timeless elegance. Its massive structure is divided into four main sections, each featuring its own beautifully designed courtyard that reflects the royal lifestyle of the rulers of Amer.

A Royal Entrance

The fort’s towering location demands a steep ascent. Visitors can make a truly royal entrance via an elephant ride, offering breathtaking panoramas of the landscape. For those seeking a traditional Rajasthani feel, a leisurely camel ride provides an equally unforgettable experience.

The 'Amber Palace' Experience

Located just 11 kilometers from Jaipur, the fort welcomes you to uncover the mysteries of its former inhabitants. Whether you are exploring the intricate carvings of its inner halls or basking in the panoramic views from the ramparts, the palace remains a crown jewel of Indian heritage.

"A harmonious blend of elegance and regal splendour."
