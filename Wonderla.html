Wonderla

India's Ultimate Fun Destination

Wonderla is India’s largest and most popular chain of amusement parks. Spanning across three major cities—Bengaluru, Kochi, and Hyderabad—it offers a thrilling escape from daily life with world-class land rides and sprawling water parks that meet international safety standards.

Wonderla Bengaluru

The flagship park and resort. It features over 60 rides, including the famous 'Recoil'—India’s first reverse looping roller coaster. It is home to a luxury resort and the iconic 'Sky Wheel' that sits atop a 13-story tower.

Wonderla Kochi

The original pioneer of the chain. Set in the lush greenery of Kerala, it is famous for its massive water park, wave pools, and the 'Wonderla Bamba' high-thrill ride, perfectly blending adventure with nature.

Wonderla Hyderabad

The most modern addition to the family. It boasts state-of-the-art attractions like 'Mission Interstellar'—a massive space flight simulation—and some of the most advanced hydraulic land rides in the country.

Global Standards of Safety

Wonderla is the only park in India to use reverse osmosis treated water in its pools and has OHSAS 18001:2007 safety certification. With over 12 million happy visitors, it stands as a testament to India's growing excellence in the leisure and entertainment industry.
